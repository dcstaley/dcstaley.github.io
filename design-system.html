Design system

Calm, professional, low-chrome. Built for clarity over decoration: no streaks, badges, or playful mascots. Light mode shown; the same tokens swap to dark later. Typeface: Inter. Comfortable density, 8px corners.

Indigo is the one UI accent (actions, selection). Teal is reserved as the "habit" identity color so tracked habits read distinctly. Two accents only, both muted.

Items are neutral by default. Color comes from a tag's color, or a custom color on an untagged item (the blue "Work" row), and fills the card. A colored left bar means status: teal for a tracked habit, red for overdue (with a red label). Status bars and fill color stack without fighting, so a Work-colored item can also be overdue.
